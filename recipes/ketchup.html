Ketchup

Description

Ketchup is a tasty tomato-based condiment. It’s great on fries or burgers. In a pinch, it’s not a bad beverage.

At the movies or at home, make a deliciously smart choice with Ketchup™!

Ingredients

  • Tomato paste
  • White distilled vinegar
  • Maple syrup
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Ground allspice

Steps

  1. Put everything in a bowl
  2. WHISK for like 2 minutes
  3. Pour into small pot at medium heat
  4. when it starts bubbling, stir for 4 mintes
  5. pour into container and let chill for a few hours
  6. put it in yr pie hole
